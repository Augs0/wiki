Don't ask to ask your question

Just ask your question.

Don't ask "can i ask a question?"

Don't ask "is anyone around?"

Instead, ask your question immediately, in the correct channel. This is good manners.

You can read more at https://www.nohello.com/

Also, try to prepare your question for the best chance of a good answer, and to respect volunteers' time. See: https://stackoverflow.com/help/how-to-ask for some advice on this.
